About Madrigal

Madrigal, originally known as Mavera, is an alternative rock band founded in 2007 by Ceyhun Kaan Karakaş, Salim Kaan Alıcı, and Emre Zeytinci in Kocaeli, Turkey. Today, the band consists of Anıl Erdem Cevizci on vocals and guitar, Kaan Alıcı on bass guitar, Sanlı Akgün on drums and backing vocals, Burak Emir Kamacı on keyboard and backing vocals, and Ceyhun Kaan Karakaş on electric guitar.

Members

  • Anıl Erdem Cevizci: Vocals and Guitar
  • Kaan Alıcı: Bass Guitar
  • Sanlı Akgün: Drums and Backing Vocals
  • Burak Emir Kamacı: Keyboard and Backing Vocals
  • Ceyhun Kaan Karakaş: Electric Guitar
